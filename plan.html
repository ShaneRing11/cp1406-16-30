CP1406 - 2016 Group Project Plan

Group Number: CP1406-16-30

Members: Edward McBain, Shane Ring, Brandon Winship, Jonathan Cutjar.

Goals

The Barrier Reef Orchestra would like to increase concert attendance and donations through the website, as both of these provide the funds required to keep the orchestra running.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be measured through recording ticket sales and donations, as well as monitoring the orchestras Facebook page for increased activity.

Target Audience

The orchestra would like the website to attract a younger audience (13-26). Where possible, text will be broken up into portions more suitible for scanning (block text will be required in places due to the content the client wants us to use).

Content

  • Home: General information about the orchestra
  • Contacts: Contact details
  • Anouncements: News on upcoming events and newsletters
  • Become a member: Forms to become a member or donate to the orchestra
  • Gallery: Photo gallery
