Become a Benefactor

With a philanthric gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you) you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months. As a benefactor you will also recieve two tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at our annual Season's Launch function.

Donations are tax deductable. For more information contact Simon McConnell 0418776961, mcconnellgray@optusnet.com.au
